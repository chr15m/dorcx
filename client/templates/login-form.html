dorcx
a peerless social network

dorcx is a social network that operates out of your email box.

It's the Sherwood Forest of the interwebs, a place where you and your merry friends can hang out free from the prying eyes of corporations, advertisers, shysters and governments. It's time to take back our internet, so pick up your bow and arrow, doff yr tricorn, and join us here in the free web.

  • Users own their own data - your content lives in your IMAP mailbox.
  • Decentralised & Distributed - anyone can run a server.
  • Secure & Private - uses GPG to sign and encrypt posts.
  • Free and Open Source - A/GPLv3 license.

Follow the project & get the source code at Github.
